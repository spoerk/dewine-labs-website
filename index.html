Our Technology

Over the last 6 years, we have developed different improvements to Bluetooth Low Energy, Zigbee, Thread, and Wi-Fi communication. Our patented technology can be easily installed on your existing devices and improves their reliability, interoperability, and efficiency.

Our firmware monitors the environment and adapts the used communication parameters. This improves communication performance while optimizing energy consumption.

Unique Selling Points

Reliability of 99.9%

Our firmware improves communication reliability to over 99.9% so that no critical messages are lost.

Full Interoperability

Our patented Cross-Technology Communication allows to exchange messages across different wireless technologies.

Additional Features

Real-time Capability

Our firmware allows to send critical messages in real time, even under harsh conditions.

Long Battery Lifetime

Our firmware optimizes energy consumption so devices can operate for months on small batteries.

Increased Range

Our firmware adapts communication parameters dynamically to significantly increase communication range.

Certified

Our firmware allows to certify devices as "wireless friendly" to indicate that they cooperate with co-located networks.

Second Chance

You can easily flash our firmware on older devices to reuse them in new applications, giving them a second chance.
