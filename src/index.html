Our Technology

Over the last 6 years, we have developed different improvements to Bluetooth Low Energy, Zigbee, Thread, and Wi-Fi communication. Our patented technology can be easily installed on your existing devices and improves their reliability, interoperability, and efficiency.

Autonomous Parameter Adaptation

Our firmware monitors the environment and adapts the used communication parameters. This improves communication performance while optimizing energy consumption.

Cross-Technology Communication

Our technology also allows to exchange messaged across different wireless technologies, such as BLE, Zigbee, Thread, and Wi-Fi. This Cross-Technology Communication allows for co-located wireless networks to coordinate to further improve reliability and efficiency.

Seamless Firmware-Replacement

Our firmware can easily be installed via firmware update on existing wireless chips. We replace the existing software to boost performance. No changes to the application necessary.
